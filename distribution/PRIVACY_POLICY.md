# trackme Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

trackme is designed to be private by default.

## What trackme Uses

trackme uses location while you record a workout. It uses that information to show your route and create a workout summary.

If you allow Apple Health access, trackme can write completed workouts to Apple Health. trackme does not read Health data.

## Data Collection and Sharing

trackme does not include accounts, advertising, or third-party analytics. Workout history is stored on your device.

## Your Choices

You can change Location and Apple Health permissions in iPhone Settings or the Health app. You can delete workouts inside trackme.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when trackme changes. The effective date above identifies the current version.
